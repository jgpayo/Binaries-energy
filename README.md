Multiplicity datasets using Gaia DR3:

- Pairs_M-dwarfs.csv:   CARMENES M-dwarf pairs. Obtained from the compilation derived from fig.~15 of Cifuentes et al. (2025).
- Pairs_Ultra-wide.csv: Ultra-wide pairs (ρ ≥ 1000 arcsec) extracted from Table 5 of González-Payo et al. (2023).
- Pairs_10pc.csv:        Pairs from the 10 pc census of stars and brown dwarfs from González-Payo et al. (2026).

Columns description:

- WDS       WDS (Washington Double Stars) identifiers. If absent, the stellar name in parentheses.
- s         Pair separation in astronomical units (au)
- M         Total star masses in solar masses (M_sun)
- |U*₉|     Binding energies in units of 10³³ J
- Planets   Information on the presence or absence of planets (Yes/No)
